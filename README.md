# TravelTide Customer Loyalty Perks Optimization

End-to-end customer analytics project focused on identifying the most effective loyalty perks to improve customer engagement and retention for TravelTide.

Using SQL, Python, and Tableau, customer demographics, booking behavior, travel patterns, and discount sensitivity were analyzed to develop a customer segmentation framework and loyalty perk recommendation strategy.

## Business Problem

TravelTide offers multiple loyalty incentives including:

- Flight Discount Voucher
- Hotel Discount Voucher
- Free Checked Bag
- Hotel Night Discount

The challenge is determining which perks generate the highest customer engagement and which customer segments should be prioritized for loyalty campaigns.

---

## Business Goal

The objective of this project was to:

- Understand customer travel behavior
- Identify high-value customer segments
- Evaluate loyalty perk preferences
- Recommend data-driven loyalty strategies
- Improve customer retention and engagement

## Key Insights

### Insight 1
Customers aged 40–49 represent the highest-value customer segment, accounting for approximately 30.8% of engaged customers.

### Insight 2
Medium-frequency travelers form the largest active customer segment and represent the strongest retention opportunity.

### Insight 3
Customers purchasing both flights and hotels demonstrate significantly higher engagement than single-service customers.

### Insight 4
Flight Discount Voucher emerged as the most preferred loyalty perk, selected by approximately 52% of customers.

---

## Strategic Recommendations

1. Standardize Flight Discount Voucher as the primary loyalty incentive.
2. Prioritize customers aged 40–49 for retention campaigns.
3. Focus engagement efforts on medium-frequency travelers.
4. Target high hotel-usage customers with Free Checked Bag promotions.
